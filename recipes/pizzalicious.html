Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a recipe on how to make a delicious California style of pizza. If you want an NYC Za recipe, wait until my next post later this month.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozarella
  • Basil

Steps

  • Add water to flour in a mixing bowl. Knead thoroughly
  • Roll out flour using a rolling pin.
  • Spread marinara sauce out thoroughly
  • Sprinkle grated cheese on pizza
  • Put in oven at 375 degrees or 450 degrees for crunchier crust
