Burritos (AKA Booritos in October)

Description

This is a recipe for vegetarian burritos. It can be modified to include cooked meats as need.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • limes (juiced)
  • 1/2 cup of spinach
  • 2-3 chopped tomatoes
  • 3 to 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 can of black beans

Steps

  1. Cut all the vegetables and fruits
  2. Drain can of beans out in a strainer; rinse thoroughly
  3. Add beans to sauce pan with 1 cup of water and a dash of salt
  4. Cook on high heat for 5-7 minutes
  5. Place tortilla on stove. Flip as needed in 30-40 second increments
  6. Build out your burrito and enjoy!
