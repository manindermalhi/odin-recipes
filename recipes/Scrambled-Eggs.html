Scrambled Eggs

Description

Start your morning out right with a high protein diet of scrambled eggs and spinach

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Baby Spinach
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Break eggs into a mixing bowl
  2. Whisk eggs thoroughly to ensure consistency
  3. Pour into a frying pan on medium-high heat
  4. Flip with spatula upon crusting
  5. Add spinach into mix. Wait until wilted
  6. Add salt and pepper
  7. Cook time should take 5-7 mintues
